## Citation

Admin. (2024). A primer on artificial intelligence. *AlphaTarget*. https://alphatarget.com/

## Key Findings

1. Accessible overview of AI concepts for non-technical audiences, covering: machine learning (systems that learn from data), deep learning (neural networks with many layers), and generative AI (systems that produce new content).
2. Explains the difference between **narrow AI** (current systems, optimized for specific tasks) and **general AI** (hypothetical future systems with human-like broad intelligence).
3. Documents the key enabling factors of the current AI moment: vast datasets, massive compute, and the transformer architecture (enabling attention mechanisms and pre-training on large corpora).
4. Practical applications highlighted: natural language processing, image recognition, recommendation systems, and generative content — all with direct implications for education and training.
