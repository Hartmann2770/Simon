## Citation

Sengar, S., & Hasan, M. (2024). Generative artificial intelligence: A systematic review and applications. Preprint. http://arxiv.org/abs/2405.11029

[Read PDF in Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/esaitch/items/3VN7Z5WS/attachment/D6RVVJRL/reader)

## Key Findings

1. Systematic review of the generative AI landscape, covering foundations (transformers, large language models, diffusion models), current capabilities, and documented applications across multiple domains including education.
2. The paradigm shift from discriminative to **generative AI** is clarified: modern LLMs can produce novel content (text, code, images) that was not in their training data, rather than merely classifying or labeling existing content.
3. In educational applications, documented uses span: content generation (lesson plans, assessment items, explanations), personalized tutoring, language learning, automated feedback, and accessibility tools (transcription, translation).
4. Key limitations identified across the literature: **hallucination** (generating plausible but false claims), **bias** (reflecting training data biases), lack of **long-term memory** across sessions, difficulty with **novel reasoning** outside training distribution.
5. The review identifies a gap between capability demonstrations and rigorous educational efficacy studies — many claimed educational benefits of generative AI lack controlled experimental evidence.
6. Future directions include multi-modal AI (combining text, image, audio), smaller on-device models for privacy-preserving education, and AI-human collaborative systems.

## Methodology

Systematic review following standard methodology (database searches, inclusion/exclusion criteria, thematic synthesis). Covers literature through 2023.

## Relevance

Provides technical and empirical grounding for AI-in-education discussions. Identifies what is actually known (vs. claimed) about generative AI applications in learning. Important for situating experimental studies (Kestin/Miller, Lira/Rogers) in the broader landscape.
