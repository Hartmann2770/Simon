## Citation

Duolingo. (2023, March 14). Introducing Duolingo Max, a learning experience powered by GPT-4. *Duolingo Blog*. https://blog.duolingo.com/duolingo-max/

## Key Findings

1. Duolingo Max uses GPT-4 to add two AI-powered features: **Explain My Answer** (post-exercise explanations of why a response was correct or incorrect, in natural language) and **Roleplay** (open-ended conversational practice with AI characters).
2. The Explain My Answer feature addresses one of the limitations of gamified spaced repetition: learners know *whether* they got something right but not *why*. Natural-language explanation closes this feedback gap.
3. Roleplay creates a low-stakes conversational practice environment that did not previously exist at scale: speaking practice with a patient, available-24/7 interlocutor who can adapt to the learner's level.
4. The announcement represents a significant commercial deployment of LLMs in a major educational product — providing a real-world case study of AI augmenting (rather than replacing) a proven spaced-repetition learning system.
5. Limitations acknowledged: AI-generated explanations can be inaccurate, roleplay characters have limited context memory, and the system works best for language learning rather than domains requiring deep reasoning.
